# Module 2 Assessments

## Knowledge Check Questions

### Chapter 1: Digital Twins for Physical AI
1. Define digital twin technology in the context of robotics.
2. Explain the benefits of using digital twins in robotics development.
3. Describe the sim-to-real gap and its implications.

### Chapter 2: Physics Simulation with Gazebo
1. What are the key parameters to tune for accurate gravity simulation?
2. Explain collision detection and response mechanisms in Gazebo.
3. How does dynamics simulation affect robot behavior?

### Chapter 3: High-Fidelity Interaction with Unity
1. What role does Unity play in robotics simulation?
2. Describe human-robot interaction scenarios in Unity.
3. Explain the trade-offs between visual realism and physical accuracy.

### Chapter 4: Simulating Robotic Sensors
1. Compare LiDAR and depth camera simulation techniques.
2. How does IMU simulation contribute to robot perception?
3. Why is sensor noise modeling important for AI perception training?

### Chapter 5: Integration - Creating Complete Simulation Environments
1. Describe the Gazebo-Unity integration architecture.
2. Explain the ROS 2 messaging integration process.
3. What validation techniques are used for integrated environments?

## Practical Exercises
1. Create a simple digital twin of a mobile robot in Gazebo.
2. Integrate Unity visualization with your Gazebo simulation.
3. Implement sensor simulation for a robot in your integrated environment.
