# APA Citation Guide for ROS 2 Nervous System Module

## General APA Format for This Module

For this module, we'll follow APA 7th edition guidelines for citing sources. Below are examples of how to cite different types of sources that are relevant to the ROS 2 module:

### Journal Articles
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of the article. *Title of Periodical*, volume(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

Example:
Macenski, S., Woodall, W., & Faconti, N. (2019). Design and use paradigm of ROS 2. *IEEE Access*, 7, 153148-153154.

### Books
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle*. Publisher Name.

Example:
Quigley, M., Gerkey, B., & Smart, W. D. (2015). *Programming robots with ROS: A practical introduction to the Robot Operating System*. Morgan Kaufmann.

### Web Pages
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Date). Title of online content: Subtitle. Website Name. URL

Example:
ROS 2 Documentation. (2023). ROS 2 Humble Hawksbill Documentation. https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/

### Conference Proceedings
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of the conference name* (pp. xx-xx). Publisher. DOI or URL

Example:
Saldana, M., & Xiao, J. (2018). ROS 2: New challenges and solutions for robotics research and education. *Proceedings of the 17th International Conference on Information Technology Based Higher Education and Training*, 1-8.

## In-Text Citations

Parenthetical citation: (Author, Year)
Narrative citation: Author (Year) stated that...

Multiple authors:
- 2 authors: (Author & Author, Year)
- 3-5 authors: (Author et al., Year) for subsequent citations
- 6+ authors: (Author et al., Year) for all citations

Multiple sources: (Author, Year; Author, Year)

## Special Considerations for Technical Content

When citing technical documentation or specifications:
- Include version numbers when available
- Include specific section or API reference if relevant
- For GitHub repositories, include the commit hash if referencing a specific version

Example:
ROS 2 Working Groups. (2023). Quality of Service in ROS 2. https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/Concepts/About-Quality-of-Service-settings.html
