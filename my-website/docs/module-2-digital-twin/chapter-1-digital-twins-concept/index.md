# Chapter 1: Digital Twins for Physical AI

This chapter introduces the fundamental concepts of Digital Twins in the context of robotics and physical AI. We'll explore the definition, evolution, and importance of Digital Twins in robotics, as well as the challenges and benefits of simulation-based development.

## Learning Objectives

After completing this chapter, you should be able to:
- Define what a Digital Twin is in the context of robotics
- Explain the evolution of Digital Twin concepts from manufacturing to robotics
- Articulate the benefits and limitations of Digital Twin technology
- Understand the sim-to-real gap concept and its implications
- Compare simulation-based testing with real-world testing approaches

## Prerequisites

Before starting this chapter, ensure you have:
- Basic understanding of robotics concepts
- Familiarity with ROS 2 (covered in Module 1)
- Fundamental knowledge of physics (gravity, collisions, dynamics)
- Academic background in engineering, computer science, or related field
